Microformat structure

We need the following data points to describe the essential data of a transaction:

Parties: 👨‍👩‍👧 h-card
  • Any number of parties
  • Needs additional data fields, in particular tax ID and VAT number
Transaction:
Type can be any of the following types (more to be added; these are the most important ones IMO):
Sale 👉 Transfer of ownership and transfer of money
Gift 👉 Transfer of ownership for free
Rent 👉 Transfer of possesion and transfer of money
Borrow 👉 Transfer of posession for free
Rendering services 👉 Rendering services and transfer of money
Object and service must be specified:
Object/service 📦 h-product
Description
Amount
Price
Terms
Terms for this transaction⚙️
Terms for all transactions⚙️
